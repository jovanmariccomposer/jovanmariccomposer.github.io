Bio

Born, studied and working in Belgrade.

Classically trained composer since early youth, with expertise in flute and piano.

Graduated from a musical high school in 2011 with a degree in music production, having done Front-of-House for dozens of live concert shows. Graduated from Faculty of Dramatic Arts with a Degree in Sound Design and Recording in 2016 with over forty student films and theatric plays in his portfolio.

From 2014 – 2016 worked as an assistant composer and arranger to esteemed theatre composer Irena Popović Dragović. From 2016 onward working in Sonic Tree Studio as a composer and music editor.
